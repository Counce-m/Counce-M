Alley Cat Allies
Redesign

User's interested in finding information that will help them get started with helping out cats in their community. They navigated to the “Take Action” page on Alley Cat Allies, but have problem finding relevant information that meet their needs. Redesigning the navigation and “Take Action” page for cat lovers who would like to help out their community cats will help them find the information they need faster and arrive at specific landing pages consistently without getting lost.

User Research Plan

Objective: Understand areas of improvement in the market space while crafting solutions to utilize in the UX of our app.

Research Methodology: Gather research from potential users through the techniques of surveys and interviews while tracking the answers and identifying themes and differences in the given answers.

User Testing-Homepage

During the survey and user interviews, we discovered that most individuals find it difficult to navigate site due to it's limited design and unclear layout

Therefore, content organziation was a must to help navigate the user to the infomation they seek. Within the design process we created a layout that allowed users to recall and arrive at the correct location consistently for the information they need. The overall experience was pleasant and not frustrating
