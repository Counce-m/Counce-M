User Research Plan

Objective: Understand areas of improvement in the market space while crafting solutions to utilize in the UX of our app.

Research Methodology: Gather research from potential users through the techniques of surveys and interviews while tracking the answers and identifying themes and differences in the given answers.

Results

During the survey and user interviews, we discovered that most individuals find it difficult to access local events in their community due to lack of resources.

Therefore, individuals who seek engagement in their community and seek to try new events within reach will greatly benefit from our app. We can assist these desires and needs with our app that coordinates and allows for users to create new events in the community

Competitor Analysis

During our competitor analysis we were able to analyze other competitors and identify strengths and pain points that users have found when navigating their site. From there we were able to consider the opportunities of the user’s feedback for features and layout of the app.

Clickable Prototype
